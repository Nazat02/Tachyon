governing relation
E² = p²c² − m²c⁴
Ordinary particles trace E² = p²c² + m²c⁴, a hyperboloid of two sheets inside the light cone. Flip the sign on m² and the surface inverts — opening into a single connected sheet that never closes near p = 0. There is no rest frame: E ≥ |p|c everywhere on it.
